## Usage

- Assigned in order as teams join the lobby (team 1 gets Red Rabbits, team 2 gets Orange Ocelots, etc.)
- Team colour is used for claimed-square highlighting on all boards
- Team name shown on claimed squares (abbreviated if needed, e.g. "Red R.")
- Primary 15 are optimised for high contrast on white backgrounds and against each other
- Backup 15 are usable but may look similar to a primary colour at a glance
